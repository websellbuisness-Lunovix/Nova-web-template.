Thank you for choosing Nova. This is a premium, zero-dependency SaaS landing page system designed for fast deployment and high-end design authority.

1. GETTING STARTED

Extract the ZIP: Unzip this folder to your computer.

Open the File: Locate index.html.

How to Edit: Right-click index.html and select "Open with..." then choose a text editor like VS Code, Sublime Text, or Notepad.

Preview: To see your changes, simply double-click index.html to open it in your browser.

2. EDITING TEXT & CONTENT

Headings: Search for <h1> or <h3> tags to change your main titles.

Paragraphs: Search for <p> tags to update your descriptions.

Buttons: Find the text inside <button> tags to change your Call-to-Action (CTA).

3. CUSTOMIZING BUTTON LINKS
To make a button go to your checkout or sign-up page, wrap it in an anchor tag like this:
<a href="your-link-here.com"><button class="btn btn-primary">Get Started</button></a>

4. EDITING THE UI PREVIEW (HERO SECTION)
To move or tilt the floating "Silver Cards" in the top section:

Find the div with class ui-mock-card.

To Move: Change the top: and left: percentage values in the style attribute.

To Tilt: Change the rotate(-5deg) value (e.g., use 5deg to tilt it the other way).

5. CHANGING BRAND COLORS
At the very top of the index.html file, find the :root section:

primary-white: The highlight color.

dark-graphite: The main background color.
Update these hex codes to match your brand instantly.
